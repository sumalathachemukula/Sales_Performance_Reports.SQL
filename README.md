# SQL Project | Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data using SQL to generate meaningful business reports and insights.  
The main objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into structured reports that help in understanding sales performance, profitability, customer payment behavior, and regional trends.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To utilize SQL for transforming raw sales data into meaningful reports that provide clarity on performance metrics and business trends.

---

## 📊 Key Reports Generated

### 1️⃣ Sales Summary Report
- Total Orders
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Average Profit

### 2️⃣ Top 5 Products Analysis
- Identified top-performing products based on revenue contribution.

### 3️⃣ Category-wise Sales Performance
- Compared sales across different product categories.

### 4️⃣ Payment Mode Analysis
- Analyzed customer payment preferences:
  - Cash
  - UPI
  - Card

### 5️⃣ Monthly Sales & Profit Trend
- Tracked monthly business growth and profitability trends.

### 6️⃣ Region-wise Sales & Profitability
- Evaluated performance across different regions.
